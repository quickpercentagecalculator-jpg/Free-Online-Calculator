What is GST?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is an indirect tax applied on the supply of goods and services. It has replaced multiple taxes to simplify the taxation system and make it more transparent.

How to Calculate GST?

GST is calculated using a simple formula:

GST = (Amount × GST Rate) ÷ 100

For example, if a product costs ₹1000 and the GST rate is 18%, then the GST amount is ₹180 and the total price becomes ₹1180.
