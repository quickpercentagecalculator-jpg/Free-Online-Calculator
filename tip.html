Tip Calculator





What is a Tip Calculator?

A tip calculator is a tool used to calculate the tip amount based on your total bill. It helps you quickly determine how much extra you should pay as a tip.

How to Calculate Tip?

Tip is calculated using the formula:

Tip = (Bill Amount × Tip %) ÷ 100

For example, if your bill is ₹500 and you want to give a 10% tip, the tip amount will be ₹50 and total bill becomes ₹550.

Why Use Tip Calculator?

  • Quick and easy calculation
  • Useful in restaurants and services
  • No manual effort required

Conclusion

This tip calculator helps you quickly calculate tip and total bill amount. It is simple, fast, and useful for everyday use.
