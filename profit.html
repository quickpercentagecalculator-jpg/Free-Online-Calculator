Profit and Loss Calculator





What is Profit and Loss?

Profit and loss are basic financial concepts used to determine whether a business or transaction is gaining or losing money. Profit occurs when the selling price is higher than the cost price, while loss occurs when the cost price is higher than the selling price.

How to Calculate Profit and Loss?

Profit = Selling Price − Cost Price

Loss = Cost Price − Selling Price

Profit % = (Profit ÷ Cost Price) × 100

Loss % = (Loss ÷ Cost Price) × 100

Example

If the cost price of an item is ₹500 and the selling price is ₹600, then profit is ₹100 and profit percentage is 20%.

Conclusion

This profit and loss calculator helps you quickly determine whether you are making a profit or loss and by how much percentage. It is simple and useful for everyday financial calculations.
