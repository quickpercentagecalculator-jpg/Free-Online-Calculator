What is Discount?

A discount is a reduction in the original price of a product or service. It is commonly used in sales and offers to attract customers and increase purchases.

How to Calculate Discount?

Discount is calculated using the formula:

Discount = (Original Price × Discount %) ÷ 100

Final Price = Original Price − Discount

For example, if a product costs ₹1000 and has a 20% discount, then the discount is ₹200 and the final price becomes ₹800.

Why Use Discount Calculator?

  • Quick and accurate calculation
  • Helps in shopping decisions
  • Saves time and effort

Conclusion

This discount calculator helps you quickly find the discounted price of any product. It is simple, fast, and useful for everyday shopping.
