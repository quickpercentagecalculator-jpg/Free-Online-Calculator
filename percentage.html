What is Percentage?

Percentage is a way of expressing a number as a fraction of 100. It is widely used in mathematics, finance, education, and everyday calculations to compare values easily.

How to Calculate Percentage?

Percentage is calculated using the formula:

Percentage = (Value ÷ Total Value) × 100

For example, if you score 45 out of 50 marks, your percentage is (45 ÷ 50) × 100 = 90%.

Where is Percentage Used?

  • Exam and academic results
  • Shopping discounts
  • Profit and loss calculations
  • Financial analysis
