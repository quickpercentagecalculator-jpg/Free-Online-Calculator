Compound Interest Calculator







What is Compound Interest?

Compound interest is the interest calculated on the initial principal and also on the accumulated interest from previous periods. It helps your money grow faster compared to simple interest.

How to Calculate Compound Interest?

Compound interest is calculated using the formula:

A = P × (1 + R/100)t

Where P is principal, R is rate of interest, and t is time in years.

Example

If you invest ₹1000 at 10% interest for 2 years, the total amount becomes ₹1210 and compound interest is ₹210.
