Simple Interest Calculator







What is Simple Interest?

Simple interest is the interest calculated only on the original principal amount. It is commonly used in loans and basic financial calculations.

How to Calculate Simple Interest?

Simple interest is calculated using the formula:

SI = (Principal × Rate × Time) ÷ 100

For example, if you invest ₹1000 at 10% interest for 2 years, the simple interest will be ₹200 and the total amount becomes ₹1200.
