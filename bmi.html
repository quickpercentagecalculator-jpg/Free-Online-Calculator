BMI Calculator





What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple calculation used to measure whether a person has a healthy body weight based on their height and weight. It is widely used as a basic health indicator.

How to Calculate BMI?

BMI is calculated using the following formula:

BMI = Weight (kg) ÷ Height² (m)

For example, if a person weighs 70 kg and has a height of 1.75 meters, their BMI would be approximately 22.86.

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI between 18.5 and 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI between 25 and 29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 or more

Why Use BMI Calculator?

  • No manual calculation needed
  • Helpful for tracking fitness
